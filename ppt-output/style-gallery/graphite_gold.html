01 · The approach

Two levers, working together

Acme Program

Productivity comes from two levers at once: AI does more of the actual work, and a new operating model removes the waits between the work. Tools alone move only the first lever — which is why tool-only teams plateau.

Platform Engineering
Working pipelines and migrations, faster — with the waiting taken out.
AI does the work
  • From intent, agents generate code, translations, and the tests that prove it — human-approved at each gate.
Remove the waits
  • No handoff queues — modelers, developers, and testers collapse into one flow.
  • No context-hunting — the knowledge layer answers instantly.
ResultThe whole intent → production cycle shrinks — not just the coding step.
Pipeline Support
Incidents resolved faster — recurring ones prevented before they page anyone.
AI does the work
  • Agents triage, find root cause, and draft the fix — recurring patterns automated before they escalate.
Remove the waits
  • No triage backlog — failures routed the moment they land.
  • No re-diagnosing — known patterns resolve automatically.
ResultMTTR drops and the recurring-failure tail shrinks — the team gets off the treadmill.
Leadership support
A named sponsor who funds the work, clears blockers, and protects the team's time.
Make-or-break
The team wants it
Genuinely interested and leaning in. If they don't want it, no amount of tooling fixes that.
